Royal Central Kitchen — Al Zahir Palace

HVAC Design, Coordination & As-Built Documentation

Project Overview

This project involved the full HVAC design and BIM coordination of a large central kitchen located in Al Zahir Palace, Jeddah. Work included duct routing, equipment placement, AHU coordination, and rooftop mechanical design — ensuring complete model accuracy and clash-free integration with architectural and structural elements.
